## Overview
This project investigates the intersection of digital security and accessibility for blind and visually impaired users. By developing a benchmarking framework for organizational profiles and digital rankings, the research evaluates how effectively major companies accommodate screen reader users and non-visual interaction standards.

## Key Features
* **Accessibility Benchmarking**: Compiles and ranks digital interfaces based on their usability for visually impaired users.
* **Company Profiles**: Provides a structured, accessible dataset of organizational profiles, highlighting their approach to digital inclusivity.
* **Research-Driven UI**: Built with a focus on accessible navigation, ensuring the benchmarking tools themselves are fully compliant with non-visual accessibility standards.
